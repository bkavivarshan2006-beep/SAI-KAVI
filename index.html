About the shop

Sai Kavi Print Hub is run as a straightforward neighbourhood counter — the kind of shop where you bring a pen-drive, a school form, or a phone that needs topping up, and walk out with it sorted in a few minutes. SK Printers stands behind every photocopy, print and laminate that goes out the door.

No queues for simple jobs, no minimum order on prints, and prices that are pinned up and kept current — what you see on this page is what you pay at the counter.

  • Photocopying — B/W & colour, A4 and A3
  • Document typing and DTP work
  • Mobile recharge / reload counter
  • Lamination — ID size to A4
  • Stationery — pens, files, notebooks & more
