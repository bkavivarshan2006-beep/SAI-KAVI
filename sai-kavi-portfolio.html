About Sai Kavi

Every cut has a reason.

I'm a video and photo editor working across cinematic storytelling, wedding films, social content, YouTube and long-form edits, thumbnails, posters and colour grading. My process starts with the story you're trying to tell, not the timeline — pacing, tone and colour follow from there.

Clients come back for the same thing: edits that feel intentional. Nothing loud for the sake of being loud. Every transition, grade and beat is chosen, not defaulted to.
